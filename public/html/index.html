About

Get to know our history

  • 2024

    The initial idea

    Through our university project, our team discovered the promising potential of an app centered on cultivating new, healthy habits rather than solely focusing on weight loss. While the app is tailored to support individuals combating obesity, its benefits extend to anyone striving for a healthier lifestyle.

  • 2025

    App is launched

    After nearly a year of testing, design and implementation our team has finally launched Vital!

  • July 2025

    Transition to full service and implementing your feedback

    Thanks to your feedback our team has been able to improve our features and add new ones as per your request. This has contributed to a more widespread use of the app!

  • 2026

    Vision and our future goals

    Our aim is to broaden the reach of Vital, enhancing our impact on children's nutritional education. We are committed to the principle that prevention surpasses treatment. To combat obesity worldwide, it is crucial to educate future generations.

A balanced plate is a happy plate!

Learn about nutrition and new cooking techniques!.

Did you know that an avocado has more than twice as much potassium as a banana? Or that the mushroom is the only non-animal natural source of vitamin D?In our weekly nutrition workshops, you'll delve deep into the world of food, exploring its diverse array of micro and macro nutrients. Gain valuable insights into how our bodies metabolize food and the role these nutrients play in fostering healthy growth and weight maintenance. Discover innovative cooking techniques and exotic ingredients sourced from around the globe. Come join us to unravel the science of food firsthand, all while indulging in delicious culinary creations!

Weight Warriors

Get support from fellow users and create your own safe space

Embarking on the journey to replace unhealthy habits with healthier ones can be an uphill battle, fraught with setbacks and feelings of guilt. Studies have shown that having a supportive community can significantly enhance one's chances of success in making lasting changes. Our weekly therapy sessions provide a safe space for individuals to connect with others facing similar challenges and navigating comparable adversities. Here, you'll find solidarity and understanding as you share tips, tricks, and support in a non-judgmental environment. It's all about fostering a sense of community where we uplift and empower each other along the journey.

Get in the groove!

Dance your way into a healthier lifestyle with us.

Exercise and physical activity often carry the misconception of being solely about grueling gym routines, sweating it out day in and day out, and struggling to maintain motivation. But it doesn't have to be that way! Achieving weight loss and embracing a healthier lifestyle doesn't mean you're confined to the gym. The key is finding activities that you genuinely enjoy and that get your body moving.Our weekly dance classes offer a fantastic opportunity to not only meet new people but also engage in a fun and fulfilling exercise session. With a diverse range of dance styles including African dance, salsa, tango, bachata, hip-hop, and more, there's something for everyone. Join us to not only get your body moving but also to socialize and have a blast while doing it!

Oot and aboot!

Enjoy nature with all the possible outdoor activities with our community.

Research indicates that spending time in nature can significantly benefit mental health, offering relief from issues like anxiety and depression. It's an opportunity to unwind, engage in light physical activity, and connect with others while discovering new landscapes.

Gentle gians forum

Enjoy our anonym support group where we lift each other up.

While the internet has often been associated with cyberbullying and harsh criticism towards those who share their lives online, it doesn't have to be this way. The online world can also serve as a valuable tool for fostering supportive communities through forums where individuals uplift each other, share recipes, and offer mutual assistance. It's about finding a community where one feels safe and supported, gaining the motivation needed to continue on the path towards cultivating positive habits.

Curvy beauty

Ladies, here's an exciting opportunity to not only receive support from fellow women but also pamper yourselves with fabulous nail and hair treatments. Connect with other female users, bond over beauty, and indulge in some well-deserved glam time!
